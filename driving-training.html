Refresher Training

At Goriola Driving School we offer a wide variety of different classes for all drivers, regardless of your driving experience and schedule. We tailor the lessons to your needs. From driving in the high way, reversing, parallel parking, mirrors and blind spots, night driving or anything you want to brush up on we will make it happen. You are in-charge of selecting the lesson you need brush up on.

Training From Crash

We understand the pressure that comes with running a tight schedule. Goriola offers the option of crash course/intensive driving lessons. The crash course/intensive driving is great when starting your lessons from scratch or just wanting to brush up on your skills. Whether you learn to drive over a period of a few months or just a few weeks, Goriola will ensure you have the right skills to be a competent driver and be a safe driver for life.

Advance Driving Training

This training is designed to develop deep understanding of advanced crash-prevention concepts and techniques, crash-mitigation strategy and tactics, practice skills associated with evasive action maneuvers during high-stress driving situations, Vehicle Inspection Techniques.

Defensive Driving Training

This is an exclusive service for drivers who want to further their driviing skills on proper road usage/management, accident prevention and overall driver etiqutte. Our courses cut across; Principles of Defensive Driving, Accident Procedure, Vehicle System Control, Motion Management, Managing Prevailing Condition, Road Traffic Signs, Signals and Markings, Road Traffic Laws, Rules and Regulations, Motor Vehicle Maintenance and Fuel Economy. e.t.c. The training is a 2 - day program with both practical and theory courses and it is not limited to light vehicles only but also available to heavy duty trucks and motor-bikes. On completion of this training, graduated drivers would be able to perceive hazards in order to take proactive steps before it is too late. This makes the driver more responsible on the road.

Corporate/Group Training

Goriola Driving School provides a wide range of Training & Certification courses for Enterprise worldwide. We create effective training solutions to drive performance, improvements and standards in real-world workplace situations. In addition to that, training allows employees to acquire new skills, sharpen existing ones, perform better, increase productivity and be better leaders.

All Our Training Courses includes Theory and Practical Classes

Theory Classes

The Theory Classes are a set of lessons that include topics regarding traffic rules, road signs & marking, principles of safe driving, how to control the vehicle, how to avoid road hazards, how to deal with road users, driving ethics, and how to face emergencies. The lessons will be supported by pictures, films, statistics, and modern educational aids in the lecture halls. The duration of the theoretical training should be determined according to the duration of the course.

  • Highway Zebra crossing
  • Highway transportation system.
  • Instrument panel drill & vehicle controls.
  • Traffic control devices.
  • Traffic laws and violations.
  • Adverse driving conditions.
  • Safety restraint systems.
  • Child safety.
  • Emergency situations.
  • Driving techniques & maneuvers.
  • Vehicle mechanics and maintenance.
  • Vehicle systems.
  • Risk management & accident prevention.
  • Impaired driving.
  • Sharing the road.
  • First Aid.
  • Road signs

Practical Classes

During the training period, the trainee will practice driving, controlling the vehicle, dealing with the road, and more. First, the trainee will practice driving on a simulator to understand the principles of driving, and then he/she will practice driving the vehicle outside. The duration of practical training is determined by the trainee level and his/her ability to control the vehicle, ensuring that the trainee can drive safely on the road.

  • Pre-entry checks.
  • Introduction to basic vehicle mechanics.
  • Instrument panel and vehicle controls.
  • Vehicle system.
  • Safety restraints.
  • Basic driving maneuvers.
  • Advanced driving maneuvers.
  • Driving in various road environments.
  • Multiple types of parking.
  • Driving in low and high volume traffic environments.
  • Expressway driving.
  • Negotiating roundabouts.
  • Reacting in emergency situations.
  • Developing positive behavior, attitude, and courtesy
