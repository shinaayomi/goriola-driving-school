Goriola Driver's License Processing

Local Driver's License Processing

We handle Local & International Driver’s License Processing for various Driver’s License Class, from Private to Commercial Driver’s License.
At Goriola Driving School, we will help you in the Application and help to prepare you passing the driving test by Vehicle Insection Officer(VIO).

After a successful registration you will be required to take an Online or Offline Mock Test to enable us process your New Drivers License as required by the Nigerian Federal Road Safety Corp (FRSC) and Vehicle Inspection Unit (VIU)

Our Local Driver's License Includes:
  • Driver’s License Certificate Processing (Nigeria)
  • 3 Years Full Driver’s License Processing (Nigeria)
  • 5 Years Full Driver’s License Processing (Nigeria)
  • 3 & 5 Year Driver’s License Renewals (Nigeria)

International Driving Permit Processing

We process International Driving Permit to enable you drive a car anywhere in the world, which simply allow you driving world-wide.

Facts is International Driver’s License is valid in all countries that have signed the 1949 Convention on Road Traffic and is recognized in many countries that are not signatories to the Convention. You may be required to have an International driver license to drive in some countries. Most of foreign car rental agencies may require you to have one to rent a car.
